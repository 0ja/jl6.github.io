Configuration & Management

$ .setup [Category-ID] [Log-Channel-ID]

Initializes the bot. Threads are created automatically for staff. Transcripts are generated on close, and idle tickets auto-archive after 24h.

.reply [msg]

Send response to user.

.close

Resolve ticket & archive.

.dashboard

View ticket logs & status.

.tag [create/view/delete]

Manage response templates.

.blacklist [User-ID]

Prevent ticket spamming.

// FAQ

Is it free?

Yes, MailBot is free forever. No monetization.

Where is data stored?

Locally on private, self-hosted Raspberry Pi infrastructure.
